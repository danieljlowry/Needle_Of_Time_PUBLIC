# Welcome to Needle Of Time!

Needle of Time is a 2D platformer developed during FGCU’s inaugural Eagle Game Jam. Players take on the role of a time traveler navigating between the past, present, and future to prevent a mysterious force from unraveling the fabric of time. The game combines platforming mechanics with a strategic time-travel system limited by an in-game energy resource, encouraging thoughtful decision-making. As players progress across the various levels, they begin uncover a deeper narrative that challenges the motives of the governing Time Administration and its leadership, blending gameplay with an evolving story about trust, control, and hidden truths.

## Story

You, a  time traveler, have been assigned the high-level mission of saving the threads of the time stream itself from a mysterious entity seeking to shatter the very fabric of time.  

However, as you continue on your journey, dark secrets buried underneath the deepest corners of time and space are revealed, many of which were never meant to see the light of day every again. These bring the entire foundation of time administration, its holier-than-thou director, the ominous time troublemaker, and even the traveler themselves into question.

Will the time traveler be able to vanquish the darkness? 
Or will the darkness encompass the past, present, and future?
Will the director be able to hold every thread together forever?
What secrets were meant to buried forever?
And which truths were lies all along?

Uncover the secrets of the time stream, its inhabitants, and much more! Happy travels!

## Controls

A: Move left <br>
D: Move right <br>
Space: Jump <br>
E*: Time travel to the past (press again to go back to present day) <br>

*Time jumps are limited by your battery level (top left), so use it wisely!
